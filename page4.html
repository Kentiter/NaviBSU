WELCOME TO CHS

COLLEGE OF HEALTH SCIENCES(CHS)

The college of health sciences houses everythin health scicenes in the university. Departments held by CHS include;

  • Anatomy
  • Haematology
  • Physiology
  • Anatomic pathology
  • Chemical pathology
  • Microbiology and parasitology
  • Medical Library
  • Medical and biochemistry
  • Nursing
  • Pharmacology and therapeutics

HOW TO GET THERE

college of health sciences can be gotten to via the college gate it self. And if you are in the campus, chs is located on the route linking the both campuses. It cannot be missed.

LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SERVICES

Standing at the center of the school opposite CHS is the schools library and information services. It is located on the route linking the both campuses. You cannot miss it.

THE NEW ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICE

Still at the center of the school, the new administrative office of the university is located just besides the school library on the road linking the both campuses. It stands out that it canot be missed.

EPS AND GST OFFICE

The EPS and GST office is also located on the route linking the both campuses and can be seen just besides the new administrative office.
