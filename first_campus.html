WELCOME TO FIRST CAMPUS

First campus holds a vast majority of the schools infrastructures and through this page, i will journey with you through some Faculties and important places you should be aware of.

FACULTY OF SOCIAL SCIENCES

It stands as the head building for everything social sciences in the school. Although standing as the head office of social sciences it how ever does not house every department. while important administrative and academic work is carried out there as you will come to find, some departments have their own buildings. Departments belonging to social science include;

  • Economics
  • Sociology
  • Mass communication(located in 2nd campus)
  • psychology
  • Political science

HOW TO GET THERE;

Although accessible through many routes in campus, the easiest descriptive route is the interlocked road besides the schools ICT office as it is a straight walk from there. The schools ICT office is located on the route linking the both campuses and the interlocked road is beside it.

DEPARTMENT OF ECONOMICS

As i mentioned earlier, not every department under social sciences has its department in the Faculty and economics department is one of them.

HOW TO GET THERE

DEPARTMENTS OF POLITICAL SCIENCE, SOCIOLOGY, PSYCHOLOGY

Although the faculty office was built and it houses some departments, these department buildings are not to be forgotten as they still serve the departments and tey are worth mentioning and knowing.

HOW TO GET THERE;

From First gate, it is a straight walk down to the round about. on getting to the round about where the statue of Father Adasu is, you take right which leads down to the road linking both campuses. On your way, theres a road besides the biology park. Taking the road will lead you to these departmental buildings which is a straight walk and is located by the right.

FACULTY OF ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCE

This is faculty houses everything environmental science in the school. Although general information anything enviromental sciences can be gotten here, they also departmental buildings which i would direct you to. Faculty of enviromental sciences have;

If you have followed up till now, i made mention of the interlocked road besides the school ICT. The faculty of environmental sciences and its departments are accessible through that route as Faculty environmental sciences is opposite the faculty of social sciencs while the departments of enviromental science are located just behind the schools ICT on the same route. You cannot miss it. Urban and regional planning and geography are close to each other.

VOCATIONAL AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION

HOW TO GET THERE

Voc and tech from first gate is a straight walk from the gate and on approaching the statue to the right of is the voc and tech department. You cannot miss it.

EDUCATION FOUNDATION

Education foundation is accessible through various routes. You can get to education foundation via the road linking both campus as it faces the maths and computer department. At the start of the road linking both campuses education foundation is to the left. That is coming from first campus. It is accessible from second campus by using the interlocked road as the department is loacted behind the urban and regional planning department.

LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SERVICES

Department of library and information sciences can be gotten to via the interlocked road besides the the directorate of ICT. The department is besides the department of Geography and urban and regional planning.
