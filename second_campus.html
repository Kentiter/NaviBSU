WELCOME TO SECOND CAMPUS

Unlike first camp, second campus is smaller and tighter with lesser infrastructures and navigating your way around second campus would not be as tricky as the first campus. Here, i would be helping you get to the faculties of

  • Law
  • Management sciences/ Accounting
  • Arts
  • Department of Mass communication
  • Pharmaceutical sciences
Along with some important places you should know from the second campus. So, let us get into it.

FACULTY OF LAW

The prestigeous faculty of law building serves as both the faculty and the department of law as everything law related is carried out in this place.

HOW TO GET THERE

From the second gate, faculty of law is accessible through two routes. you can get to faculty of law by taking a straight walk through the right divide of the tall erected class rooms. going straight until yo pass the departments of management sciences, past the boys hostel and football pitch and then the faculty is up ahead. It can also be gotten to by following the road on the extreme right of the gate, the curved road leads straight to the faculty.

Visit google maps for further assistance

MANAGEMENT SCIENCES/ ACCOUNTING

This building houses everything management sciences and accounting in schoool.

HOW TO GET THERE

The faculty of management sciences and accounting just like mentioned in the direction to first camp is a straight walk from the second gate. You can take either the left or right side of the tall buildings which stands out. It is a striaaght walk that cannot be missed.

FACULTY OF ARTS

This faculty building houses everything art in the university. It houses departments of;

  • English
  • History
  • Linguistics/languages
  • Religion and cultural studies
  • Philosopy
  • Theatre arts

HOW TO GET THERE

The faculty of arts is located just behind the faculty of manangement scicences and accounting building. It is a straight walk from the second gate past the the faculty of management sciceneces.

DEPARTMENT OF MASS COMMUNICATION

Although part of the faculty of social sciences, the department of mass communication is located in the second campus of the university.

HOW TO GET THERE

From second gate, taking the road to the extreme left of the of the campus, a straight walk will lead you to the department of mass communication.

visit google maps for more assistance

DEPARTMENT OF PHARMACEUTICAL SCIENCES

The newly established faculty of Pharmaceutical sciences is located in second campus and hopefully, it will be operational soonest.

HOW TO GET THERE

From second campus, the faculty of Pharmaceutical sciences is located on the road linking both campuses.

Places in second campus you should know include

THE COMPUTER CENTRE

The school computer centre is located just besides the faculty of Pharmaceutical sciences.

THE UNIVERSITY BOOKSHOP

It is a straight walk from second gate. It is exactly straight ahead.

ACADEMIC PLANNING

The directorate of academic planning is located at second campus and it is a straight walk from the gate to the left of the campus. or simply it is behind the bookshop.
